Introduction. I am not your grandma but…

In a similar way as David Cain, writer of Raptitude, and its post on unsolicited life advice, here is a quick list of my habits and practices for a good life.1 The pieces of advice evolve from more to less work related.2

  1. If you want to know more, I have a blog where I post different types of things from time to time. You may find something useful there as well. Carlito Fluito Website. One of my current life goals is to become a full-time artist, including writing, and posting regularly. Good vibes and support is always welcomed.↩

  2. This is the classical “I am not a doctor” message. Use your common sense, take all this practices as a personal exploration sort of game, and never put your well-being under extreme situations or circumstances. If you are like me, you probably don’t care and you’ll end up doing what you want, but at least, I will sleep better knowing that I wrote this small warning.↩
